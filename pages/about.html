About StrataSight

Built for
the builder

Most privately-held businesses never get access to the depth of strategic analysis that institutional players take for granted. StrataSight exists to change that — bringing rigorous, data-driven advisory to the businesses that need it most.

Our Philosophy

Depth over
decoration

Every business has more enterprise value potential than its owners typically realize. The gap between where a company is and where it could be is almost always operational — a product of process gaps, cost structures, and strategic blind spots that accumulate quietly over years.

StrataSight was built to find that gap and close it. We don't traffic in generic frameworks or recycled playbooks. Every engagement starts with your actual data and ends with a report built specifically for your situation.

Our scope is intentional and narrow. Strategic advisory at the business level — operational clarity, value assessment, and growth direction. That focus is what lets us go deep rather than wide.

"The gap between what a business is worth today and what it could be worth is almost always operational — and almost always closeable."

— StrataSight Advisory Team

How We Operate

Core principles

I

Evidence Only

Every recommendation is grounded in your actual financial and operational data. Not gut feel. Not industry averages loosely applied. Your business, analyzed on its own terms.

II

Defined Scope

We operate strictly at the corporate strategy level. Business operations, valuation, and growth planning. This keeps our analysis clean, our recommendations sharp, and our engagements clear.

III

Built to Use

Deliverables designed to be acted on — not filed away. Prioritized findings, specific next steps, and measurable outcomes. We stay engaged through implementation when the engagement calls for it.
