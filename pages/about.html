Our Philosophy

StrataSight was built to find that gap and close it. We don't bring generic frameworks or recycled playbooks. Every engagement starts with your actual data and ends with a report built for your specific situation.

We sit in a deliberate position in the advisory landscape: between where you operate today and where your financial planner takes the business next. Our work feeds directly into that handoff — cleaner numbers, higher value, better outcomes.

Core principles.

Evidence Only

Every recommendation is grounded in your actual financial and operational data. Not gut feel, not industry averages loosely applied. Your business, analyzed on its own terms.

We work strictly at the corporate strategy level — business operations, valuation, and growth planning. That keeps our analysis clean and our recommendations sharp.
