Services

Three ways we
move the needle

Every StrataSight engagement maps directly to a driver of enterprise value — from understanding where you stand, to clearing what's holding you back, to building a path forward.

01

Strategic Value Assessment

Most owners carry a number in their head about what their business is worth. Without rigorous, data-backed analysis, that number is almost always wrong — sometimes significantly. Our assessments deliver a disciplined, evidence-based picture of your business's current enterprise value, alongside a clear map of every factor driving it up or holding it back.

We analyze financial performance trends, market comparables, customer concentration, operational risk, management depth, and growth trajectory to build the complete picture most owners have never seen. This is a working document you can use — not a ballpark.

  • Enterprise value opinion
  • Comparable market benchmarking
  • Value driver identification and ranking
  • Gap analysis and opportunity map
  • Multiple analysis (EBITDA, SDE, revenue)
  • Risk factor assessment
  • Written report and strategy walkthrough
  • 90-day check-in available
02

Operational Efficiency Consulting

Inefficiency rarely announces itself. It shows up as steady margin compression, departments that feel busy but don't produce, and overhead that keeps growing without proportional output. Left unaddressed, it compounds year after year — and gets priced in at exit.

We conduct a structured operational review across your key cost centers, workflow systems, and profitability metrics. The output is a ranked list of specific improvements with estimated impact — so you know exactly where to focus first.

  • Profitability analysis by product or segment
  • Cost structure review and benchmarking
  • Workflow and resource allocation audit
  • Margin improvement modeling
  • Productivity and output analysis
  • Ranked recommendations with estimated impact
  • Implementation sequencing guide
  • Follow-up review milestone available
03

Strategic Growth Advisory

Growth without a strategy is just spending faster. Our growth engagements are built around your specific business model, market dynamics, and operational capacity — not frameworks borrowed from unrelated industries or companies at a different scale.

The output is a growth roadmap designed to be executed — prioritized, sequenced, and tied to real value creation milestones. Everything is strictly business-level: corporate strategy, market positioning, and operational scalability.

  • Market opportunity and competitive analysis
  • Revenue growth modeling and scenario planning
  • Customer and channel expansion strategy
  • Operational scalability assessment
  • Strategic initiative prioritization
  • ROI framing for major decisions
  • Roadmap with clear milestones
  • Ongoing advisory retainer available
