Services

Three ways we
move the needle

Every StrataSight engagement maps directly to a driver of enterprise value. From understanding where you stand, to eliminating what's holding you back, to building a clear path forward.

01

Strategic Value Assessment

Most business owners carry a number in their head. Without rigorous, data-backed analysis, that number is almost always wrong — sometimes significantly so. Our Strategic Value Assessments deliver a disciplined, evidence-based opinion of your business's current enterprise value, alongside a clear map of every factor driving it up or pulling it down.

We analyze financial performance trends, market comparables, customer concentration, operational risk, management depth, and growth trajectory to build the complete picture that most owners have never actually seen. This isn't a ballpark — it's a working document you can use.

  • Non-certified enterprise value opinion
  • Comparable market benchmarking
  • Value driver identification and ranking
  • Gap analysis and opportunity map
  • Multiple analysis (EBITDA, SDE, revenue)
  • Risk factor assessment
  • Written report + strategy walkthrough
  • 90-day check-in available
02

Operational Efficiency Consulting

Inefficiency rarely announces itself. It shows up as steady margin compression, departments that feel busy but don't produce, and overhead that keeps growing without proportional output. Left unaddressed, it compounds year after year — and it gets priced into your business at exit.

We conduct a structured operational review across your key cost centers, workflow systems, and profitability metrics. The output is a ranked list of specific improvements — with estimated impact — so you know exactly where to focus first.

  • Profitability analysis by product, service, or segment
  • Cost structure review and peer benchmarking
  • Workflow and resource allocation audit
  • Margin improvement modeling
  • Productivity and output analysis
  • Ranked recommendations with estimated impact
  • Implementation sequencing guide
  • Follow-up review milestone available
03

Strategic Growth Advisory

Growth without a strategy is just spending faster. Our Strategic Growth Advisory engagements are built around your specific business model, market dynamics, and operational capacity — not frameworks borrowed from unrelated industries or companies at a different scale.

All recommendations are strictly business-level: corporate strategy, market positioning, and operational scalability. The output is a growth roadmap that is designed to be executed — prioritized, sequenced, and tied to real value creation milestones.

  • Market opportunity and competitive landscape analysis
  • Revenue growth modeling and scenario planning
  • Customer and channel expansion strategy
  • Operational scalability assessment
  • Strategic initiative prioritization
  • ROI framing for major decisions
  • Clear go-forward roadmap with milestones
  • Ongoing advisory retainer available

A note on scope: StrataSight's services are limited to business-level strategic consulting. We analyze companies — not personal financial situations. None of our services constitute personal financial planning, investment advice, insurance recommendations, tax preparation, or legal services. All value assessments represent non-certified advisory opinions only and should not be used as certified appraisals for legal, tax, or transactional purposes.

The Process

From first call
to final report

Step 01

Discovery

30 minutes. We learn your business, your goals, and where you feel the biggest gaps are. Direct and focused — no prep work required on your end.

Step 02

Data Collection

Financial statements, operational metrics, and market context. Scoped precisely to your engagement so the analysis that follows is accurate and complete.

Step 03

Analysis

Rigorous modeling across valuation drivers and operational levers. Specific to your industry, your business model, and your actual situation — not generic benchmarks.

Step 04

Delivery

A written report plus a live walkthrough. Findings, ranked recommendations, and a clear roadmap — built to be acted on, not filed away.
